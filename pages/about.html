Our story

Why we built a finance app that looks like a notebook

Most budgeting apps overwhelm you with dashboards. We wanted the quiet honesty of a paper ledger — just slightly faster.

How it started

A habit borrowed from our grandparents

Before banking apps existed, households kept a small notebook by the door — every rupee spent, written down the same day. That habit rarely failed anyone. Ledger & Leaf is our attempt to bring that discipline back, without asking you to carry a pen.

We stripped away everything that makes finance apps feel like spreadsheets: no jargon, no hidden fees, no endless settings. Just a form, a table, and two charts that tell you the truth about your month.

Milestones

From class project to daily habit

Phase 1

The first paper prototype

Sketched as a single form and table — log an amount, pick a category, see a running total.

Phase 2

Charts added

A category breakdown and a 7-day trend line turned a flat list into something worth checking daily.

Phase 3

Filters and validation

Category filtering and proper form checks made the ledger reliable enough to trust with real numbers.

Phase 4

The site you're on now

A full home, about, services, and contact experience built around the same paper-ledger spirit.

What we believe

Three rules we don't break

🪶

Stay light

If a feature doesn't help you understand your spending faster, it doesn't belong here.

🔐

Your data is yours

Everything you enter stays in your own browser. We don't have a server to lose it from.

🌱

Built to be kept

A ledger only works if you return to it. We design for the second visit, not just the first.
