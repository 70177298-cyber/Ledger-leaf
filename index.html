Why Ledger & Leaf

Built like a ledger, not a spreadsheet

No clutter, no logins, no learning curve. Just an honest record of where your money goes.

✍️

Log in seconds

Add an amount, pick a category, and you're done. The form remembers today's date so you don't have to.

📊

See the shape of your spending

A category breakdown and a 7-day trend line turn raw numbers into a picture you can actually act on.

🔍

Filter what matters

Jump straight to food, transport, or any single category when you want to dig into one part of your budget.

🔒

Stays on your device

Your ledger lives in your browser's local storage. Nothing is uploaded, sold, or shared.

📱

Works everywhere

Phone, tablet, or desktop — the layout adapts so you can log an expense the moment it happens.

🗑️

Easy to correct

Made a mistake? Delete any entry in one tap, with a confirmation so nothing vanishes by accident.
